# Governance Domain

Purpose: study claim discipline, gates, authority boundaries, evidence ledgers, and safety constraints.

Allowed claims: local policy statements, gate evidence, blocked-action mappings, lineage records.

Blocked claims: safety proof, security proof, legal authority, autonomous authorization.

Required evidence: policy source, gate result, blocked actions, claim boundary, lineage link.

Safe study modes: policy cards, failure cards, lineage cards, governance tests.

Handoff surfaces: `reports/nexus_domain_intelligence_report_latest.json`, `state/nexus_lineage_manifest_latest.json`.

Compiler gates: `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 domain`, `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 evolve`.

Claim boundary: governance study is not legal, safety, or security certification.
