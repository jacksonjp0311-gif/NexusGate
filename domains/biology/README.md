# Biology Domain

Purpose: study biological concepts as sourced, bounded patterns for adaptation, systems behavior, and evidence discipline.

Allowed claims: sourced biological concepts, clearly marked hypotheses, non-clinical educational summaries.

Blocked claims: medical authority, diagnosis, treatment advice, unsafe wet-lab instructions, unsupported biological facts.

Required evidence: source/provenance, safety boundary, uncertainty status, claim type.

Safe study modes: literature mapping, concept cards, analogy cards, non-actionable systems comparison.

Handoff surfaces: `reports/nexus_domain_intelligence_report_latest.json`, `domains/biology/`.

Compiler gates: `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 domain`, `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 evolve`.

Claim boundary: biology study is not medical authority and must not provide unsafe wet-lab instruction.
