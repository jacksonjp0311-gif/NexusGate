# Physics Domain

Purpose: study physical constraints, field patterns, dimensional consistency, simulations, and derivations.

Allowed claims: dimensionally checked claims, sourced physical principles, simulation results with assumptions.

Blocked claims: physics analogy as physical law, simulation as empirical proof, unsupported physical claims.

Required evidence: dimensional consistency, derivation/simulation status, source/provenance, assumptions.

Safe study modes: dimensional analysis, simulation cards, derivation cards, bounded analogy.

Handoff surfaces: `reports/nexus_domain_intelligence_report_latest.json`, `domains/physics/`.

Compiler gates: `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 domain`, `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 evolve`.

Claim boundary: physics study is not empirical proof or physical law certification.
