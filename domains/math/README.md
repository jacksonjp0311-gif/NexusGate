# Math Domain

Purpose: study formal structures, equations, derivations, proof sketches, and conjectures.

Allowed claims: definitions, derivations, proof-status-labeled statements, explicit conjectures.

Blocked claims: conjecture as theorem, proof by analogy, unsupported formal equivalence.

Required evidence: derivation, proof status, assumptions, definitions, or explicit conjecture status.

Safe study modes: equation cards, derivation notes, proof-status tracking, formal analogy mapping.

Handoff surfaces: `reports/nexus_domain_intelligence_report_latest.json`, `domains/math/`.

Compiler gates: `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 domain`, `.\scripts\nexus.ps1 evolve`.

Claim boundary: math study does not make a conjecture into a theorem without proof.
