# NEXUS PetriDishPortal

PetriDishPortal interlinks NEXUS GATE with the external PetriDishPro organism-gate workbench.

External root:

```text
C:\Users\jacks\OneDrive\Desktop\PetriDishPro
```

NEXUS root bridge:

```text
PetriDishPro/
```

## Surfaces

- Electron HUD mini preview: live Petri particle/cell preview from the latest Petri receipt.
- Electron `OPEN`: launches the full PetriDishPro Electron microscope HUD as a separate maximized window.
- Spiral Core Portal `[15] PetriDishPortal`: terminal access to Petri open/run/test/receipt/artifact surfaces.

## Boundary

PetriDishPro remains its own organism-gate app. NEXUS may reflect Petri state and open fixed Petri surfaces. NEXUS may not turn Petri into arbitrary shell authority, clinical authority, wet-lab authority, species-identification authority, or autonomous repo mutation authority.

## Claim Boundary

PetriDishPro previews are deterministic simulation evidence only. They are not microscopy evidence, clinical evidence, treatment guidance, species identification, biosafety evidence, or proof of biological truth.
