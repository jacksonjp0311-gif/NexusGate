# NEXUS Coherence Field Protocol

The Coherence Field is the v2.x continuity surface for NEXUS GATE.

It compresses the active operating state into one field packet:

```text
origin + decision envelope + memory + runtime pressure + wounds + certificates + policy + git scope -> coherence field
```

The field can guide routing, reduce rehydration cost, and make stale evidence visible.

It cannot authorize mutation.

## Coherence Inputs

- Origin Seal
- Decision Envelope
- Algorithm Cards
- Discovery Cards
- Policy Kernel
- Evidence dependency scaffold
- Wound intelligence scaffold
- Benchmark scaffold
- Git scope

## Coherence Score

```text
score = 100
  - origin drift penalty
  - decision drift penalty
  - missing surface penalty
  - dirty scope pressure
  - card readiness penalty
```

The score is a routing signal, not a truth certificate.

## Law

```text
coherence may guide routing
coherence may not grant authority
```

In v2.1, Decision Envelope consumes the field through the Recommendation Arbiter. The field becomes causal as pressure, not authority.
