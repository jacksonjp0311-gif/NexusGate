# Triadic Geometric Lattice Design

NEXUS GATE v2.5.0 routes recommendations through a triadic lattice:

- Evidence: packet freshness, source hashes, and admissible reports.
- Geometry: GitNexus impact pressure, hot-file pressure, and blast radius.
- Authority: governed command surfaces and non-escalation boundaries.

The lattice does not choose authority. It produces a bounded adjustment for the arbiter so NEXUS can prefer routes that are fresh, low-blast, and human-authorized.

Core law:

```text
alignment = cuberoot(evidence * geometry * authority)
```

This is deliberately multiplicative. A route cannot be excellent if one axis collapses. High geometry cannot compensate for missing authority; high authority cannot compensate for stale evidence.

Claim boundary: local development routing evidence only. No autonomous authority, no proof of correctness, no production readiness claim.
