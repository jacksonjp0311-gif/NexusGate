# Adaptive Coherence Protocol

Adaptive coherence is not one broad confidence number.

v2.7.0 measures:

- identity integrity;
- causal integrity;
- experience integrity;
- calibration integrity;
- sample sufficiency.

The score is a conservative geometric mean. Low sample sufficiency keeps emergence claims blocked even when structural readiness is high.

Adaptive coherence may guide attention. It may not authorize execution or replace final gates.
