# Governed Calibration Protocol

Calibration is the plasticity plane.

A Learning Receipt may propose calibration, but calibration remains:

- explicit;
- human authorized;
- single-experience bound;
- replay-safe;
- bounded by maximum update size;
- reconstructable from ledger events.

v2.7.0 records calibration authorization and holds single-experience updates. One verified experience may inform. Several independent verified experiences may influence. Repeated evidence across contexts may promote.
