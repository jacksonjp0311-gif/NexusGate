# Action-Bound Final Evolve Protocol

A general latest evolve pass is not proof of a specific action.

Action-bound final evolve records:

- target action ID;
- input receipt hashes;
- post-action observation ID;
- epoch verification result;
- action-chain verification result;
- effect receipt hash;
- whether the verifier changed canonical source.

Verifier-generated reports are separated from target-action effects. The verifier may prove local evidence freshness; it may not authorize another action, calibrate, or claim production readiness.
